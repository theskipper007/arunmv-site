<Tldr hint="the short pour">

I built a spreadsheet to optimize my coffee, learned the optimum was **"pay attention,"** and had a great time anyway.

</Tldr>

Not everything needs a return on investment. Some weekends are just for tinkering.

## The setup

Scale, timer, a notebook, and far too many variables: dose, ratio, grind size, water temperature,
pour pattern. I logged every cup for a week like it was a science fair project.

## The finding

The single biggest factor was simply paying attention — <mark>being consistent and present beat any
clever tweak.</mark> Which is, annoyingly, the lesson of most optimization.

A reminder that the personal stream here is exactly that: low-stakes, curiosity-driven, and
cleanly separated from the work writing.
